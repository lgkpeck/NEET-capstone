### Document Key Features and Drawbacks <strong>How should we document our design?</strong> We will document our design in 4 different ways. First, these design notebooks should demonstrate the design process throughout the semester. Next, we are creating a final video describing and pitching our design. We will pair this final video with a design review presentation for our partners at National University of Mongolia. Finally, we will create a technical design review document that will be added to the class website. Of these 4 types of documentation, I am most focused on the desing review report. I want to address roughly 4 aspects of the design. <br>1) Material properties of the solar salt used, including calculations of volume of solar salt needed <br>2) Discussion of shape and materials of brick, ideally with COMSOL modelling of brick temperature over time <br>3) Description of insulation box created, including modelling of how insulation thickness will impact the temperature of the brick <br>4) Overview of heating, transportation, and handling of the molten salt brick <strong>What visuals can we use to pitch it to stakeholders?</strong> I think modelling visuals in COMSOL and Fusion can help show the thermal profile of the brick.
